OUR PROGRAMS

Creating Lasting Impact

Through our comprehensive programs, we address the root causes of poverty and create sustainable pathways to prosperity for communities worldwide.

Community Skills Empowerment

Community Skills Empowerment Program (CSEP)

CSEP is our flagship initiative focused on equipping young people with practical skills that enhance employability and expand career awareness beyond traditional pathways.

  • Computer literacy and basic digital skills
  • Online research and career exploration skills
  • Career guidance beyond traditional pathways
  • Basic home maintenance training

Guiding Young Leaders

Youth Development & Mentorship

Our mentorship programmes support young people in building confidence, leadership skills, and informed decision-making for their future.

  • Personal mentorship
  • Leadership and communication skills training
  • Life skills and goal-setting guidance
  • Career and education decision support
Get Involved

Supporting Vulnerable Children

Support for Child-Headed Households

We provide home-based support to child-headed households to ensure safety, stability, and access to essential services.

  • Provision of basic household necessities
  • Psychosocial and emotional support
  • Educational and school-related assistance
  • Referral to social and community services
Get Involved

Caring for Our Elders

Home-Based Care for the Elderly

We support elderly individuals living alone or without adequate family support through compassionate, community-based care services.

  • Basic home-based care and daily assistance
  • Regular wellness and safety monitoring
  • Emotional support and companionship
  • Community engagement and referrals
Get Involved

Area of Operation

We operate primarily in Malalane and the surrounding communities within the Nkomazi Local Municipality, located in the Mpumalanga Province of South Africa.

Our community-based approach allows us to build strong local partnerships and deliver programmes that respond directly to the needs of the people we serve.

Primary Focus Area

  • • Malalane
  • • Surrounding communities
  • • Nkomazi Local Municipality
  • • Mpumalanga Province
