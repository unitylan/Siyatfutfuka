Who We Are

Siyatfutfuka Welfare Center was established to address the social and economic challenges faced by rural communities in and around Malalane. We focus on empowering young people with practical skills, supporting child-headed households, and providing care for the elderly.

Our approach is community-driven, sustainable, and rooted in lived experience.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable youth, families, and elderly persons in rural communities through skills development, mentorship, and essential social support.

"Our organisation exists for those voices."

What We Do

Our programmes are designed to empower young people and support vulnerable households through skills development, mentorship, and community-based care.

Why It Matters

Understanding the challenges faced by rural communities helps us create meaningful, long-term solutions.

Many rural youth pass matric with strong results but lack exposure, guidance, and access to resources that can help them transition into meaningful opportunities. Others face poverty, trauma, or social challenges that limit their ability to reach their full potential.
