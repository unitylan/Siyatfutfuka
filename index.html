Who We Are

Our NGO Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to creating just and equitable societies. Our mandate is to mobilise resources and partnerships to contribute to positive change globally.

We are active facilitators of peace, human rights, and democracy. Our work is rooted in memory, dialogue, research and advocacy - always aligned to our commitment to freedom and dignity for all.

Our Mission

From preserving extensive archives to convening dialogue on today's most pressing issues, we use our legacy as a living resource to inspire change, advance equity, and build inclusive platforms for justice.
