ABOUT US

Our Story of Hope

For nearly three decades, we have been dedicated to transforming lives and building resilient communities around the world.

Our Mission

To mobilise resources and partnerships to contribute to the creation of just and equitable societies globally. We work tirelessly to advance peace, human rights, and democracy through advocacy, dialogue, and direct action.

Our work is rooted in memory, dialogue, research and advocacy - always aligned to our commitment to freedom and dignity for all.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to justice, equality, and opportunity. We envision communities where human rights are respected, democracy thrives, and peace prevails.

Through our programs and partnerships, we strive to build inclusive platforms for justice and inspire positive change across the globe.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to create lasting change, our NGO Foundation has been at the forefront of social justice and human rights advocacy for over two decades. What started as a small initiative has grown into a global movement, touching the lives of millions.

We are the custodians of important legacies and active facilitators of change. Our work spans from preserving extensive archives to convening dialogue on today's most pressing issues. We use our platform as a living resource to inspire change, advance equity, and build inclusive platforms for justice.

From preserving historical records to convening critical dialogues, we leverage our resources to address systemic challenges and create opportunities for marginalized communities worldwide.

Our Values

Justice

We believe in fair treatment and equal opportunities for all, regardless of background or circumstance.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and a deep commitment to improving lives and communities.
